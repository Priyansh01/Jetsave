THE JETSAVE STORY

    JETSAVE INDIA TOURS PVT. LTD HAS BEEN INTO OPERATIONS OF VISA FACILITATION SINCE 1990 AND EVER SINCE ITS INCEPTION OUR TEAM HAS GIVEN UTMOST IMPORTANCE TO CLIENT’S SATISFACTION. WITH OVER 28 YEARS OF RICH EXPERIENCE OF VISA CONSOLIDATION, JETSAVE HAS BUILT UP EXCELLENT WORKING RELATIONSHIP WITH ALL THE CONSULATES AND HIGH COMMISSIONS IN INDIA AND HAS EARNED THE REPUTATION OF BEING THE MOST RELIABLE AND LEADING VISA FACILITATION COMPANY OF INDIA. MOST OF OUR STAFF MEMBERS I.E. SENIOR VISA EXECUTIVES, FRONT OFFICE EXECUTIVES AND FIELD EXECUTIVES ETC. ARE ASSOCIATED WITH US FOR OVER A DECADE AND ARE WELL AWARE ABOUT THE PROCEDURES AND VISA FORMALITIES FOR ALL COUNTRIES ACROSS THE GLOBE. JETSAVE STARTED ITS OPERATIONS IN DELHI AND OVER LAST 28 YEARS, IT HAS SPREAD ITS WINGS ACROSS NATION WITH HEAD OFFICE SITUATED IN DELHI & BRANCHES AT BANGALORE, KOLKATA, GURGAON, MUMBAI, PUNE, HYDERABAD & CHENNAI. ALL OUR BRANCH OFFICES ARE FULL-FLEDGED AND FULLY FUNCTIONAL WITH A TEAM OF EXPERIENCED VISA EXECUTIVES SERVICING OUR CLIENTS. BESIDES THIS, JETSAVE HAS NETWORKED WITH THE TRAVEL AGENTS IN EVERY CITY/TOWN HAVING CONSULATES. AS ON TODAY, JETSAVE CATERS TO OVER 4000 TRAVEL AGENTS AND CLIENTS ACROSS COUNTRY AND HAS A TRACK RECORD OF FACILITATING OVER 2,50,000 (TWO HUNDRED AND FIFTY THOUSAND) VISAS IN A YEAR.
